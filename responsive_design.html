responsive design

solution description

How often do you look at a screen? My guess is very often, and I even think you switch between different screens such as a mobile, TV and computer screen. Because there are so many different screens, it also means that the content must be able to adapt, so the user gets a good experience no matter where the user opens a website. When you work with making your website responsive, you can work according to different methods, where I have used mobile first, which means you structure everything according to a mobile screen, and further work up larger screens such as tables and computers. The better content can adapt to different screens, the better experience, which is super important when there are so many websites

The solution

details

There are various ways to make your website responsive. On the website here I have used flexbox on the menu to to place content, most responsively as possible, and grid on my first section because I want the text to flow an entire column and image to fill the second.
